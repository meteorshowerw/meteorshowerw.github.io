All the steam and early electric locomotives of a great railway system. in a volume that includes particulars of some 10.000 motive power units. Formed in 1905. the Italian State Railways inherited a varied collection of old locomotives. The age of these was such that, presented here in effect. is a history of Italian locomotive practice over the past 130 years. The great number of illustrations includes many drawings, the latter for the benefit of model-makers reproduced to a uniform scale of 3.5 mm/ft. As well as steam locomotives, the story of threephase electric motive power units is covered.
