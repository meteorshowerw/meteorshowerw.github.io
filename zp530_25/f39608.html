Art ornaments inspired by The Met collection.

Tiffany (American, 1848–1933) for Tiffany Studios (American, 1902–32), the elaborate florals on the Museum"s lamp, reinterpreted on this luminous cloisonné ornament, illustrate the designer"s obsession with natural beauty.
